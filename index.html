The Wax

We blend coconut and rapeseed waxes — never paraffin. Our proprietary formula burns 40% cleaner and holds fragrance up to 30% longer than standard waxes.

The Vessel

All vessels are made from recycled glass. Mail them back using our prepaid label and we'll clean and refill them for 20% off your next purchase.

The Packaging

Boxes are printed on FSC-certified recycled card with soy-based inks. The tissue paper is seeded with wildflowers — plant it, don't bin it.

The Shipping

We've partnered with EcoCart to offset 100% of shipping emissions. Since 2022, we've offset 1,240 tonnes of CO₂ equivalent.

Candle
Care

A well-tended candle burns brighter, longer, and more evenly. Here's how to treat yours.

01

First Burn

The first burn is the most important. Allow the wax to melt to the edges of the vessel before extinguishing — this prevents tunneling and sets the memory for every burn after.

02

Trim the Wick

Before each burn, trim the wick to ¼ inch (6mm). A long wick causes excess smoke, soot, and uneven burning. Use scissors or a dedicated wick trimmer.

03

Burn Time

Burn for no more than 4 hours at a time. Beyond this, the wick mushrooms, the wax pool overheats, and the fragrance dissipates less effectively.

04

Extinguishing

Use a candle snuffer rather than blowing out the flame. Blowing distributes soot into the wax pool and can shift the wick off center.

05

Storage

Store away from direct sunlight in a cool, dry place. UV exposure causes fragrance oils to oxidize and discolors wax. The lid is not decorative — use it.

06

End of Life

When about ½ inch of wax remains, retire the candle. Pour boiling water into the vessel, pour it out, and mail the vessel back for our refill program.
